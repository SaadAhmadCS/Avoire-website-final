The Experience of
Avoire Atelier

Elysian Noir is not merely a fragrance; it is a meticulously curated atmosphere. Each bottle is the result of eighteen months of extraction and maceration, utilizing rare botanicals harvested at the peak of their aromatic potency.

The scent opens with a sharp, crystalline clarity—the Calabrian Bergamot provides a luminous entry before surrendering to the velvet depths of the Damask Rose. It is a fragrance that evolves with the wearer, reacting to skin temperature to reveal hidden layers of smoke and spice throughout the evening.
